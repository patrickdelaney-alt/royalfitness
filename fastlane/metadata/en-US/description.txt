Royal is the all-in-one fitness app for people who take their health seriously.

TRACK YOUR WORKOUTS
Log every exercise with sets, reps, weight, and perceived exertion. Build your own workout library, replay past sessions, and watch your strength grow over time.

TRACK YOUR NUTRITION
Log meals and recipes with automatic macro and calorie breakdowns. Build a personalised meal library and hit your daily nutrition targets with clarity.

TRACK YOUR WELLNESS
Log meditation, yoga, sleep, and other wellness activities. Monitor your daily steps, discover new wellness habits, and build consistency day by day.

BUILD YOUR COMMUNITY
Follow friends, share workouts, comment on posts, and celebrate each other's progress. Join gyms, create group challenges, and train together — even remotely.

EARN ACHIEVEMENTS
Unlock badges as you hit milestones. Stay motivated with a gamified progress system that rewards consistency.

SUPPLEMENTS & ACCESSORIES
Keep track of your supplement stack and wellness accessories in one place. Share what works with your community.

Royal keeps everything you need to perform at your best in one clean, beautifully designed app. Start your journey today.
